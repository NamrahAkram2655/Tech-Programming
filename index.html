Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tech?
The Ministry of Information Technology & Telecommunication (MoITT) has a vision to provide Freelancing Training to empower youth of the nation. In order to bring this vision to verity, a comprehensive program named National Tech Training Programme (Tech) has been designed. This program provides contemporary training to aspiring freelancers across all provinces of Pakistan. This program aims to empower 22,000+ individuals to be self-sustainable, through its 20 state-of-art centers.
